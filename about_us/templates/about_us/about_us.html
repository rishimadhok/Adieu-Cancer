About Us

We're on a mission to free the world from cancer. Until we do, we'll be funding and conducting research, sharing expert information, supporting patients, and spreading the word about prevention. All so you can live longer and better. While most people know us for our research, we do so much more. We attack cancer from every possible angle.

We promote healthy lifestyles to help you prevent cancer. We research cancer and its causes to find more answers and better treatments. We have even made a unique, one of its kind cancer predictor, which is quite accurate (upto 99%) for all of you so that you can, at your convenience determine whether your cancer is benign and malignant within seconds.

We fight for lifesaving policy changes. We provide everything from emotional support to the latest cancer information for those who have been touched by cancer. And we do it all 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Join us, and together we can make a difference in today’s workplace while fighting cancer and saving lives.
